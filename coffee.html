About Us.

The Velvet Bean was born from a quiet dream and a deep love for the little things. Founded by a girl who believed that life’s softest moments live in warm cups and slow conversations, this space was once just a notebook sketch — a vision scribbled between pages of poetry and coffee-stained thoughts. After years of gathering vintage mugs, collecting stories from quiet cafés around the world, and perfecting the kind of brew that feels like a hug, she opened these doors with one simple intention: to create a home for wandering souls, tired hearts, and anyone who needs a pause. Today, The Velvet Bean stands not just as a café, but as a love letter to stillness, comfort, and the art of slowing down.
